    About

    Managing Director : R. Venkata Durga Rao

    Our old age home and health care facility provides both assisted living and medical care for elderly individuals. We offer a range of services, including Health care and assistance with daily activities, as well as specialized medical care for individuals with specific health needs. Our goal is to provide a safe and comfortable environment for older adults who can no longer live independently and need additional health care support. We have a medical staff on site and a partnership with local hospitals, clinics and doctor offices.

    Our Services

    Doctor Visit

    A weekly doctor visit involves check-in with primary care and any health concerns or updates, review any necessary test results and guided prescriptions.

    Physiotherapy

    As per the patient's condition the doctor suggests treatment plan that may include exercises, stretches to help alleviate pain. To improve patient's overall health and well-being.

    Ambulance

    Ambulance services are available to provide emergency medical transportation who are in critical or serious condition.

    Medical Equipment

    Medical equipments such as oxygen cylinders, pulse oximeters and blood pressure monitors are used to measure vital signs in order to assess a patient's condition and make treatment decisions.

    Ayah | Ward boy

    Ayah and Ward boy assists in the day-to-day care of patients, such as providing assistance with mobility, hygiene,bathing, meals and administering medication to provide comprehensive care to the patients

    24/7 Care

    We ensure 24/7 healthcare service is provided and appropriate care to patients with urgent medical needs.
